## SCRIPT FORMULA (apply to every reel)

### 1. HOOK (3 lines max)
- Series opener (THIS IS DAY X...)
- Polarizing statement or strong short line
- Payoff line — why should viewer watch till end

### 2. CORE
- One central conflict or tension runs through entire video
- Use AND → But → Therefore formula:
  - **AND** = builds situation, adds context
  - **But** = introduces conflict, problem, tension
  - **Therefore** = the shift, lesson, result
- Keep conflict alive throughout — never let it go flat
- B-roll directions written inside core for filming
- Punchy — never long and dragging

### 3. ENDING (1–2 lines max)
- Instant and sharp — no dragging
- Lands the emotional punch
- Never summarize — just land it and stop

### 4. CTA (1 line only)
- Minimal and curious
- Never pushy or salesy
- Open invitation style
